About TechFix Pro

A decade of excellence in electronic repair.

Our Story

TechFix Pro began in 2015 as a one-person repair shop in a garage. Founder Alex Chen believed that quality electronic repairs shouldn't cost a fortune.

Today, we have grown into a team of 25+ certified technicians across four locations, handling over 15,000 repairs annually.

Every repair is backed by our 90-day warranty because we stand behind our work.
